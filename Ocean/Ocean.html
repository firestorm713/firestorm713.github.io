Unity Web Player | Ocean Shader Experiments

The primary points of this were to mess with various parts of shaders and to start working toward using Tessendorf's Ocean Wave simulation system. Currently, what it does is calculates longitudinal and transverse waves based on sine for y direction and a cosine wave for the x direction. The plane isn't simply animated, but is displaced according to a height map. For each pixel on the height map, the normal and tangent are calculated, and passed into the shader. The transparency and color are also based on the height, and are calculated on the fly.
