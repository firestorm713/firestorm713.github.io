« created with Unity »

Controls:
Steer with the mouse, Forward/Reverse - W/S, Left/Right - A/D
Fire - Left Mouse Button
This was our semester project for Video Game Design. I worked on this in a group with Austin Stoner and Cullen McDevitt. I was in charge of making the Asteroids deform with Marching Squares. Each Asteroid is defined by a two dimensional array, which indicates how much each point on the asteroid is damaged. As the player hits the asteroids, they deform.
